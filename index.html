About Me

Olga Levina joined Craft Culinary Concepts in October 2016 as Marketing Manager. Originally from Russia, Olga has traveled around the world and studied at schools in St. Petersburg, London, and later New York. Upon graduating with a business administration degree in 2013, Olga started her career in New York as a real estate professional, gradually transitioning into PR and marketing.

Olga has extensive experience in sales, print and digital marketing, website design and development, graphic design, photography, managing social media and guest analytics for industry specific solutions. Olga also manages the guest feedback program for Craft Culinary through multiple social media platforms such as Trip Advisor and client based guest management programs.
